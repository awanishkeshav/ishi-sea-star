CloudMate

Comprehensive coverage with real-time wide area surveillance solution using high-resolution 4kcameras

Overview

Cloud Mate connects to most 4K UHD cameras as well as existing 720p network cameras, and the Eye4k visualization software, or existing Video Management Systems(VMS) software when longduration, forensic video-review is required.

CloudMate passes through 720P camera video while at the same time performing buffered recording of up to 512GB of 4K UHD video.

CloudMate and the Eye4K visualization software provide short-term video review capability at 4K UHD resolution while displaying live video at 720P resolution.

How it Works

ADVANTAGES

Enables real-time and post-event assessment and analysis

Integrates with most POE 4K and standard network cameras

Wireless or Wired interface for flexible deployment

Eliminates the need to install new high-bandwidth video infrastructure to exploit 4K UHD video

Dramatically reduces ongoing over-the-air data transmission costs

TECHNICAL SPECS

Compatibility

  • Supports Power-Over-Ethernet(POE) off-the-shelf cameras: Axis, Arecont, Avigilon, Sony
  • Compatible with most Video Management System(VMS) and Eye4K Visualization Software
  • Seamleass pass-through port connection to camera

Connectivity

  • POE for Camera
  • RJ45 Ethernet
  • USB 2.0
  • WiFi with external antenna
  • Support external over-the-air data Aircard
  • 3.5mm stereo Audio Input

Storage

  • Online access to 10 seconds of buffered 4K UHD video
  • Buffered storage and off-line access to 512Gb of 4K UHD video - up to 2 days

Power & Dimensions

  • 48VDC, 0.625A (110VAC adapter included)
  • 6.25” x 4.5” x 2.5”
